  • The first display of its kind

    Now you see it, now you don’t

    Interact with information in a new way with the integrated visual display on Meta Ray-Ban Display. The full-color, high-resolution 600x600 pixel display built into the right lens of the glasses enables you to view messages, images and video, notifications, text and visual answers to your queries and more without having to take out your phone. Visible only to you and appearing and disappearing at your command, the display is there when you want and gone when you don’t, leaving nothing but a clear lens.

  • Intelligence on display

    Meta AI with visual responses on Meta Ray-Ban Display can help you solve problems you encounter in your daily life, gain a greater understanding of a subject, retain more information and quickly move through your day, effortlessly.
    Ask Meta AI on Meta Ray-Ban Display and get help as you go, hands-free. As you work, step-by-step instructions can appear on the in-lens display, allowing you to track each step, chart your progress and confirm you’re using the proper technique with the help of visual references.
